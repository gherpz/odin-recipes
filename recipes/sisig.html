Sigsig

filipino pork sisig


Ingredients

  • Pork Ears
  • Chicken Liver
  • 2-3 Whole Onions, diced
  • Salt
  • Pepper , Ground
  • Soy Sauce
  • Kalamansi / lemon
  • Cooking oil
  • Mama Sita's Sisig Mix

Directions

Prepping the main Ingredients

  1. Boiled the pig ears for 1 hour with garlic, salt and pepper
  2. dry it and put in the oven 350 for 45 mins
  3. dicely chop the pork

Cooking

  1. saute the onion, 1 head
  2. mixed the pork with the onions
  3. add the spices
  4. pour mama sita Sauce
  5. mix all together
Enjoy !
